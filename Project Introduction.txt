All Around Security Manager App
*******************************

This security manager application will be the solution to all your security needs. 
From password storage, password generation, real-time protection, and two-step authentication. 
This application will put you at ease knowing all your information is safe and encrypted. 
The application will be easy to use and accessible to all people. 
It will protect and/or store all important information and leave you without worry. 
The application is designed to add protection and convenience to its user's information on any device they wish to use it on.
